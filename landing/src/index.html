Transfer native and ERC20 coins to multiple accounts in a single transfer.

Pick the amount and currency that you want to send

Add all the addresses that you want to transfer to

Execute smart contract

The contract will divide the payment equally and transfer to all the payees

Use cases

Equally splitting payments between users

You can split a payment between multiple users, even add yourself to make the system calculate the amount for you

Automatically split transfers

Integrate the interface into your smart contract and automatically split payments that you receive
